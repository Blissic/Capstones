How the Internet Works

Computers connect together to form networks. The Internet is just a huge network of computers all around the world!

Fun Fact: The first message sent over the Internet was "LO" - they were trying to send "LOGIN" but the system crashed!

Sending Information

When you send a message or watch a video, the information gets broken into small pieces called "packets" that travel through wires or through the air.

Fun Fact: Some internet cables run under the ocean between continents!

Wi-Fi Magic

Wi-Fi uses invisible radio waves to send information through the air, just like how radios work but much faster!

Fun Fact: The word "Wi-Fi" doesn't actually stand for anything - it was just a catchy name!
